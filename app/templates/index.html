Benign or Malignant - Insight

Benign thyroid tumors are non-cancerous and usually grow slowly. Malignant tumors are cancerous, can grow quickly, and may spread to other parts of the body. Identifying whether the tumor is benign or malignant is critical for timely treatment planning.
